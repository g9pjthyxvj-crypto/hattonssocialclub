Your $100 deposit secures your appointment and goes directly toward the cost of your tattoo. Deposits are non-refundable but fully transferable if you reschedule with at least 48 hours notice.

Final pricing is discussed during your consultation and depends on size, complexity, and placement. Your artist will give you a full quote before any ink touches skin.

Our Story

Built on Trust.

Hatton's Social Club was founded in 2019 on a single belief — that the relationship between an artist and their client is sacred. When someone sits in our chair they're trusting us with something permanent. We don't take that lightly.

We hand-picked five of LA's most respected tattoo artists — each a master of their style — and built a studio where world-class work happens every single day. No flash mill. No rushing. Just permanent art created with intention.
