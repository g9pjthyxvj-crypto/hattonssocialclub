Our Story

Built on Trust.

Inkhouse was founded in 2018 on one belief — that the relationship between artist and client is sacred. When someone sits in our chair, they're trusting us with something permanent. We take that seriously every single day.

We hand-picked five of the most respected tattoo artists on the Central Coast and built a studio in Santa Cruz where world-class work happens every day. No flash mill. No rushing. Just permanent art created with intention.
